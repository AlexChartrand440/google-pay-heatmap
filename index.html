Download your My activty.html

Unfortunately there is no API to retrieve your Google Pay activities. Complete the following steps:

  • Login to takeout.google.com
  • Click the 'SELECT NONE' button
  • Scroll down to Google Pay and click the toggle.
  • Click the radio button 'Select activity and saved items'
  • From the little modal only select 'My activity' and then 'OK'
  • Scroll down to the bottom of the page and select 'NEXT'
  • On the next page click 'CREATE ARCHIVE'
  • Wait on the screen until the data is available for download
  • Finally, download the archive and drag & drop the My activty.html file
